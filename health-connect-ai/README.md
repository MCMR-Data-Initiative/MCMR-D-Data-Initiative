# Health Connect AI

Health Connect AI is a digital health application developed under the Mother & Child Malaria Response – Data Initiative (MCMR-D).

The application is designed to support data-driven healthcare, community health outreach, malaria prevention, maternal and child health, and improved access to health information.

## Live Application

Visit the Health Connect AI application:

## MCMR-Data Initiative

The Mother & Child Malaria Response – Data Initiative (MCMR-D) aims to reduce malaria-related illness and deaths among pregnant women and children under five through data, digital tools, community outreach, early detection, and inclusive health solutions.
