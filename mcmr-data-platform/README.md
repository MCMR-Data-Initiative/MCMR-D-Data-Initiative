# MCMR-Data Platform

This digital platform supports the Mother & Child Malaria Response – Data Initiative (MCMR-D).

The initiative uses data, digital tools, community outreach, and innovation to support malaria prevention and improve health outcomes for pregnant women and children under five.

## Pilot Location

Uhunmwode Local Government Area

## Key Focus Areas

- Malaria prevention and awareness
- Early testing and detection
- Maternal and child health
- Community health outreach
- Data collection and monitoring
- Data-driven decision-making
- Micro-insurance inclusion
